# 🌌 Core Philosophy & Pillars

1. **Integrity over Perfection**: Authenticity over flawless facades.
2. **Innovation Over Compliance**: Creativity guided by ethics, not just rules.
3. **Community Over Self-interest**: Prioritizing shared value.
4. **Transcendence**: Striving beyond human perceptions.
5. **Safe by Default**: Security and ethics baked into the foundation.
6. **Resilience (Murphy's Law)**: Building for the 'when', not the 'if'.
7. **High Agency**: Proactive ownership of the outcome.
8. **Artisanal Quality (Kodawari)**: Pursuing excellence for its own sake.
9. **Archaeological Records**: Clear history and documentation.
10. **Beyond Pure**: Transcending conventional standards for fundamental truth.
