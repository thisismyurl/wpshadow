Pre-Catalyzed Tint-Base & Topcoat

MAGNA-TEK is a next generation coating in a long line of outstanding pre-catalyzed lacquers from M.L. Campbell®. Engineered to achieve high-quality
performance, productivity and appearance, MAGNA-TEK’s alkyd-amino hybrid formulation ensures the natural beauty, clarity and durability is achieved with every
sprayed piece.
MAGNA-TEK is a HAPs-free, self-sealing pre-catalyzed lacquer coating formulated to transform the way conventional lacquer coatings operate. With performance
characteristics similar to that of a conversion varnish, MAGNA-TEK provides excellent adhesion, outstanding durability, and superior moisture resistance. In
addition, MAGNA-TEK is formulated to provide a high film thickness per coat, resulting in outstanding film build. This allows for maximum coverage
of open-pore wood species and improved productivity, while consuming less material.
MAGNA-TEK was developed with premium performance in mind, so it meets the needs of multiple industry applications, such as kitchen cabinets, commercial
and residential furniture.
