

# **WPShadow Social Media Strategy** 

## **Our Content Promise (what we want non-followers to feel)** 

WPShadow helps WordPress site owners understand what’s happening with their site, make one small improvement at a time, and recover calmly if something doesn’t go as planned. 

## **Primary Conversion Action** 

- Soft, friendly   
- Install WPShadow Core \> then point visitors to a KB / quick guide / checklist as the next step (aligned with “teach first, then invite them to check out a resource” approach)

### 5 Content Pillars (Weekly Rotation) 

1. **Calendar Dates & Site Ops Dates (signature lane):** Use WPShadow 2026 Social Media Calendar Dates as recurring hooks (backup day, privacy week, Global Accessibility Awareness Day, etc.).   
2. **1-minute WordPress Wins:** Tiny improvements for followers’ sites that feel doable.   
3. **Explain It Like I’m Busy:** Plain-language WordPress concepts, not talking down to visitor but simply explained.   
4. **Fix It Safely:** Backup/rollback mindset \+ Murphy’s Law recovery paths   
5. **People \+ Community: (**Christopher Ross \+ Allison Smith \+ other team spotlights; open source gratitude. 

#### **Monthly Mix** 

- Feels helpful while promoting the product.   
- Simple guideline that aligns with “advice, not sales.”   
  - 60% educational   
  - 25% entertaining / relatable   
  - 15% product / releases   
- Promotional posts use this structure: What changed \> why it matters \> what to do next (1 small step)

## **Network-Specific Content Playbooks (what to post and how it should look)** 

### **Reddit (priority)**

**Cadence:** 3–5 helpful comments/day, 1 post/week  
**Formats that work:**

- Calm checklists  
- “If this were my site…” troubleshooting  
- “Here’s a safe first step” responses

**Weekly post ideas (templates):**

- “The 10-minute site check I do every week”  
- “Scheduled posts missed? Try these 5 checks (no panic)”  
- “Backups: the part everyone skips (restore test)”

**Soft CTA (only after providing real help):**  
 “WPShadow Core can help you spot issues like this with local diagnostics—no account needed.”

(Keep naming correct: **WPShadow Guardian** is local and part of Core.)

### **Facebook Page (priority)**

**Cadence:** 4 posts/week \+ 1 weekly “routine”  
**Best formats:** carousels, short screen captures, “Site Care Saturday”

**Series:**

- **Site Care Saturday:** 1 habit \+ why \+ 1 next step  
- **Calendar Dates Clinic:** 1 scheduling/time-related fix per week  
- **Quick wins:** “Pick one today” style (mirrors the Observance pack tone)

### **Discord communities (priority)**

**Cadence:** 2 short “value drops” per week \+ 1 monthly office hour  
**Best formats:** pinned checklists, short clips, Q\&A threads

**Ideas:**

- “Troubleshooting flowchart: what to check first”  
- “Office hours: bring one WordPress weirdness; we’ll pick 3 and walk through calmly”  
- 

### **YouTube**

**Cadence:** 2 long videos/month \+ 4–8 Shorts/month  
**Video types:**

- “Fix with me” screen recordings  
- “Explainers” (WP-Cron, timezones, scheduled posts)  
- Release walkthroughs (what changed \+ why it matters \+ what to do next)

Tie March content to “Academy & Training” so videos naturally become your training library.

### **Instagram**

**Cadence:** 3 Reels/week \+ 2 carousels/week  
**Best formats:** “3 steps” carousels \+ 20–40s Reels with captions  
Use Observance pack graphic prompts \+ alt text patterns. 

### **X / Twitter**

**Cadence:** 4–6 posts/week \+ 1 thread/week  
**Best formats:** threads, quick checklists, polls  
Use “one idea \+ one action” posts; avoid promo-heavy language.

### **LinkedIn**

**Cadence:** 2–3 posts/week  
**Best formats:** mini case studies, product philosophy, process (how you design for accessibility/learning)  
This is where “trusted advisor” shows up strongly.

## **30/60/90-day execution plan (fits 7–10 hours/week)**

### **First 30 days: set the foundation**

**Goal:** consistent posting \+ repeatable templates

- Build 6 post templates (checklist, myth-bust, “fix safely”, observance post, release post, team spotlight)  
- Record 8 short screen captures (20–40s) you can reuse everywhere  
- Create a “Calendar Dates Clinic” weekly slot using the PDF pack

### **Days 31–60: deepen authority**

**Goal:** searchable evergreen content \+ community presence

- 2 YouTube videos (scheduled posts/timezones \+ backups/restore test)  
- 1 Reddit “mega checklist” post pinned to your profile  
- Start monthly Discord office hours

### **Days 61–90: scale what works**

**Goal:** turn best posts into series \+ update playbooks

- Double down on top 2 topics by saves/comments  
- Add 1 lightweight community ritual: “Weekly WordPress Win” threads  
- Prepare April observances early (World Backup Day is a great anchor)
