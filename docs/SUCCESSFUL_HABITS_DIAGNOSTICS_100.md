# 100 Behavioral Diagnostics: Best Habits of Successful Website Owners

**Purpose:** Test the behaviors, practices, and habits that correlate with successful websites and thriving businesses.

**Format:** Each diagnostic returns true/false or pass/fail based on measurable behavioral indicators.

**Philosophy:** Success isn't just technical - it's about consistent habits, strategic thinking, and commitment to continuous improvement.

## 🎯 Implementation Notes

### **Testability**
Each diagnostic should:
- Return true/false or pass/fail
- Check for measurable evidence of the behavior
- Provide context about what's missing
- Suggest concrete next steps

### **Scoring**
- Create an overall "Success Habits Score" (0-100)
- Calculate by category
- Show trends over time
- Benchmark against industry

### **Threat Levels**
- Low (20-40): Nice to have, improves efficiency
- Medium (40-65): Important, impacts results
- High (65-85): Critical, significant impact
- Severe (85-100): Essential, business at risk without

### **Recommendations**
For each diagnostic, provide:
- Why this habit matters
- How to implement it
- Industry benchmarks
- Tools that help
- Quick wins vs long-term
