# KB Pipeline and Feature Ideas

This doc captures future KB articles and WPShadow feature ideas sparked while drafting content. Update as we write more.

## KB Article Ideas (to prioritize later)
- Archive vs Delete: Safely archive content instead of removing it, with redirects and audit logging.
- Instant Deletion Workflow: Configure workflows for safe hard-deletes with pre/post checks and KPIs.
- Restore from Vault Snapshot: Step-by-step restore of posts/pages from WPShadow Vault backups.
- Redirect Builder for Retired Content: Map deleted URLs to new destinations to preserve SEO.

## WPShadow Feature Ideas
- Archive Instead of Delete (Core/Pro): One-click archive action with status change, redirect helper, and audit log entry.
- Vault Pre-Action Snapshots: Prompt to take a WPShadow Vault backup before destructive actions (delete, bulk operations).
- Post-Delete Redirect Helper: Suggest/create 301 redirects for deleted or archived posts to preserve SEO.
- Kanban Hooks for Content Changes: Auto-create Kanban cards for delete/archive/restore actions with owner and timestamp.
- Safety Workflow Template: A reusable workflow that snapshots → executes action → clears cache → runs health checks.
