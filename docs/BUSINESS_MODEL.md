# WPShadow Business Model

**Date:** February 4, 2026  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Status:** ✅ Active & Enforced

> **WPShadow is built to help first, sell second.** The business model exists to keep the project sustainable while protecting users from paywalls, pressure, or surprises.

---

## Our Core Commitments

### 1) Free as Possible
If a feature can run on your own server without ongoing costs, it stays free.

**Free Forever (Core Plugin):**
- WPShadow Guardian (local diagnostic monitor)
- All local diagnostics
- All automatic fixes (with undo)
- Dashboard, Kanban board, activity log
- Workflow automation
- KPI tracking

### 2) Register, Don’t Pay
We ask for registration only when we pay real, ongoing costs to provide a service. Think of it like a library card—free to sign up, but required so we know how many resources to maintain.

**Example:** Cloud diagnostics require servers and third‑party APIs (like renting office space and hiring security guards), so they use a free tier + paid upgrades for heavier use.

### 3) Advice, Not Sales
We teach first. We never use scare tactics or hidden limits.

**Good behavior:**
- Explain why a change matters in plain language
- Offer free learning links
- Show how to fix issues without paying

### 4) Privacy First
Your data stays on your server unless you opt in.

**What this means:**
- No cloud calls without consent
- Clear notices before any external scan
- Transparent about what data is sent and why

## Release Cadence for Paid Products (2026)

We release paid products in a **foundation‑first order** (like building a house—foundation before walls) so you never buy a tool that lacks what it needs to work properly.

**Planned cadence (monthly, end‑of‑month):**
- **March 31:** WPShadow Pro platform (licensing + module manager)
- **April 30:** WPAdmin Core (primary admin foundation)
- **May onward:** Secondary WPAdmin modules begin monthly releases
- **July 31:** Media Hub (primary media foundation)
- **August onward:** Media Image, Video, and Document modules begin monthly releases

This schedule keeps upgrades predictable and ensures every module lands after the system it depends on.

## Why We Charge (Only When Needed)

We only charge when there are ongoing, real costs. Examples include:
- Cloud servers and storage (like renting warehouse space to store your backups)
- Third‑party scanning services (like hiring external security guards to patrol)
- Email delivery and monitoring services (like paying for a phone line)
- Priority support (human time—our team personally helping you)

We do **not** charge for:
- Basic plugin features
- Learning how WordPress works
- Access to our architecture or APIs
- Community support

## How This Protects Users

### Transparent Pricing
We explain what costs money and why. No surprises.

### Safe by Default
All risky actions have clear warnings, backups, and undo options.

## The 4 CANON Pillars (Non‑Negotiable)

These are the foundations we never compromise on:

1. **Accessibility First** — Works for people with disabilities (screen readers, keyboards, etc.)
2. **Learning Inclusive** — Clear, friendly explanations for all skill levels (everyone can learn)
3. **Culturally Respectful** — Works worldwide in any language (respects all cultures)
4. **Safe by Default** — Protects you from mistakes and malicious attacks (always has your back)

---

## Sustainability Without Pressure

We earn trust by making the free version excellent. Paid offerings exist only when we provide services that cost money to run.

**In plain terms:**
- Local Guardian = free, always
- Cloud Guardian = optional, costs money to run
- Pro Modules = for advanced workflows and teams

---

## Transparency Promise

Every paid product must clearly state:
1. What it costs
2. Why it costs that amount
3. What you get
4. What you don’t pay for
5. How the money is used

---

## Summary

WPShadow is built like a helpful neighbor: we help first, educate clearly, and only charge when we have real costs to cover. The business model is designed to protect users, keep the project sustainable, and stay true to our core philosophy.
