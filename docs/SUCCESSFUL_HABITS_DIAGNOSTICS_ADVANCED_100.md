## 💡 Key Differentiators

**What Makes These "Advanced":**

1. **Proactive vs Reactive:** These measure anticipation and preparation
2. **Strategic vs Tactical:** Focus on long-term positioning
3. **Excellence vs Adequacy:** Bar set higher than "good enough"
4. **Systematic vs Ad-hoc:** Formalized processes and programs
5. **Data-Driven vs Intuition:** Metrics and evidence-based

**Success Indicators:**
- Not just "doing things" but "doing things well"
- Not just "having systems" but "optimizing systems"
- Not just "being compliant" but "exceeding standards"
- Not just "surviving" but "thriving"
